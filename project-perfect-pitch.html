Perfect Pitch: Soccer Shot-Tracking Technology

About Perfect Pitch

Perfect Pitch is an app designed to assist soccer players improve their game without needing anything more than a ball. We came up with the idea with widespread accessibility in mind; not everyone has access to a soccer field or a goal to practice. With Perfect Pitch, you wouldn't need access to anything other than a soccer ball and a smartphone. The camera tracks the soccer ball and gives the user tips to improve their shot speed, spin, and other shot statistics. Our project was to illustrate a proof of concept for this application, shown in the GIF above; the camera is able to find and track the ball, both while stationary and in motion.

Ball-Tracking Process

1. Start capturing video in a low resolution to improve efficiency.

2. Reduce noise in the capture by making the video grayscale, then applying a Gaussian blur.

3. Get a list of all found circles in the image.

4. Of these circles, select the circle closest to the previous frame's circle.

5. Draw the selected circle's center point and circumference to outline the ball, and show the frame on the screen.
